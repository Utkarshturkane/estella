About us

How it started?

Hitesh Keswani

(Founder)

Hitesh Keswani, owner of Silver Beach Entertainment and Hospitality Pvt Ltd (SBEHPL), believes that his success lies in finding the right locations, building the right team, maintaining personal connections with guests, and consistently delivering top-notch service and food.

Keswani started his career as a base-line manager with brands like Mocha and Hawaiian Shack in Mumbai, at the age of 24. He quickly made a name for himself by opening his first outlet, Silver Beach Cafe in Juhu, Mumbai in 2010. He followed this up with Nom Nom in Versova and Bandra, Copa in 2013, Sirocco and Radio Bar in 2015. He expanded to Ministry of Salads and The Terrace in Mumbai in 2016, and then opened the iconic Estella in December of the same year. Estella is now Mumbai's most sought-after beach-front restaurant and bar. In 2017, he expanded to Goa with The Lazy Goose, followed by Silver Beach Cafe Dubai and Lokhandwala, Mumbai in 2018.

Keswani's company manages villas in Canary Farms Lonavla, Alibaug and Silver Beach Villa, Assagao, Goa. In 2019, he opened Basanti in JBR, Dubai and Radio Bar’s second outlet in Chembur, Mumbai. In August 2021, he launched the popular Dubai-based Greek restaurant Opa Kipos at Worli, which quickly became one of the city’s top-ranking restaurants. In just 12 years, SBEHPL has achieved a turnover of 200cr, making Hitesh’s hospitality venture a success story.

Keswani is always taking on new challenges, experimenting with different genres and formats. His ability to ideate, nurture scalable brands, sustain market changes, and create multiple value propositions has held him and the company in good stead. His company has won multiple honors, including the Times Food Award for COPA and Nom Nom, The Elite Magazine Award for Estella, and the Times Food Awards for The Lazy Goose in Goa.

At 37 years old, Keswani has plans to open a Japanese restaurant in Mumbai and Radio Bar in Seawoods, Vashi, indicating that there is no stopping this enterprising entrepreneur.
